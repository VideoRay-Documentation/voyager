Equipment Guide

VoyagerExplorer ROV

The VideoRay VoyagerExplorer is used for very basic diving operations and is ideal for training beginners. The VideoRay VoyagerExplorer weighs 3.6 kgs (8 lbs), has a depth rating of 76 m (250 ft), a fixed color camera, and 50 mm propellers.

VoyagerExplorer ROV

Control Panel

The control panel provides power, communications and a video interface between the surface and the ROV through the tether. The control panel has a small 7 inch color LCD display and knobs controlling horizontal and vertical motion of the sub as well as the halogen lighting. It weighs 6.8 kgs (15 lbs).

Tether

The tether connects the ROV to the surface and provides power, communications, video and an Auxiliary Pair of Independent Conductors (APIC) for accessory use. The tether consists of conductors, a KevlarÂ® strength member, flotation (for Neutral and Performance tethers) and an outer jacket. The VoyagerExplorer comes with 40 m (130ft) of thin diameter professional performance tether. It comes wrapped as pictured with the VoyagerExplorer and coiled on the Tether Deployment Systems (TDS) with the VoyagerExplorer X3.

Tether

Tether Deployment System (TDS)

The TDS (Tether Deployment System) is an optional device that consists of a tether reel within a case. This system allows the tether to be managed neatly, and helps avoid tether tangles. The Tether Deployment System (TDS) with 40 m (130 ft) of tether is included with the VoyagerExplorer X3.

VideoRay VoyagerExplorer Tool kit

The basic tool kit is delivered with both the VoyagerExplorer and includes a 10 in 1 tool, spare O-Rings, lubricant, spare nuts, screws, and other necessities.
